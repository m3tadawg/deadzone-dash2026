Shambler: Features basic tattered clothes and a few scars.

Runner: Distinctive lean build with dynamic hair, a missing eye, and a frantic expression.

Brute: The largest of the set, a formidable heavy-duty zombie wearing reinforced armor.

Crawler: A unique 'prone' design on a chest sled, covered in grime and tattered rags.

Spitter: Notable for its distended jaw and acidic drool.

Screamer: The largest head, a 'Leader' with an elongated throat and a distinct high-collar trench coat.

Plague Lord: A decaying master of contagion, draped in tattered robes, a gas mask, and a unique bubbling chest feature.

Colossus: The true Titan of the group, a monolithic zombie in bulky power armor that has seen better days.
